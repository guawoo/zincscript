What Does Mobile Phone Look Like in Future?

Posted by JarodYv Wed, 10 Dec 2008 04:25:00 GMT

CNET asked frog to design the ultimate phone of the future, incorporating the features CNET readers craved.  frog envisioned a phone that not only delivers unsurpassed technology but becomes an essential extension of the user – “ubiquitous” to the modern user”s life and connection to the world.  Presenting… UBIK.

UBIK is a sleek tour de force of a phone no bigger than a 3x3 post-it notepad.

The heart of UBIK is the flexible, borderless, foldable high-resolution screen. The casing is two hard shells that are OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) screens that can light up any image. 

UBIK features state of the art mobile access, the ability to play music and games in countless formats, and a high-end camera with liquid optical zoom lens.  An ambient light cue can be programmed to monitor the weather, the stock market, incoming calls, or any number of functions. An optical scanner ensures you are the only one who can access your UBIK. 

With its intricate design details, layered functionality and virtually limitless customizability, the phone of the future embodies “maximalism.” 

Read the cnet article to learn more about UBIK, the indelible companion for the well-connected human of the future.
